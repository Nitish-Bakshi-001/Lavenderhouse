About the House

Where the day
arrives slowly.

Lavender House is a single-residence boutique villa — designed for those who know the value of stillness. Every surface, every hour, every arrangement has been considered with one intention: to let you rest deeply.

Set off Airport Road in Amritsar, the house sits behind a garden wall, quietly removed from the city's pulse. You'll arrive to chilled lavender water, warmed linen, and nobody else's schedule.
